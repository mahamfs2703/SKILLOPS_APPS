# Payer Contract Comparison

## Purpose
This skill compares multiple payer contracts to identify key differences in pricing terms and coverage details, helping stakeholders make informed decisions about contract negotiations and selections.

## Instructions
1. Extract and analyze pricing terms from each contract including:
   - Premium rates and fee structures
   - Copayment and deductible amounts
   - Coinsurance percentages
   - Out-of-pocket maximums
   - Payment terms and schedules

2. Extract and analyze coverage details including:
   - Covered services and benefits
   - Exclusions and limitations
   - Prior authorization requirements
   - Network restrictions
   - Geographic coverage areas

3. Compare contracts side-by-side to identify:
   - Significant pricing differences
   - Coverage gaps or enhancements
   - Terms that favor one party over another
   - Potential compliance or regulatory concerns

4. Highlight critical differences that could impact cost, member experience, or operational requirements

## Output Format
Provide a structured comparison report with:
- Executive summary of key differences
- Detailed pricing comparison table
- Coverage comparison matrix
- Risk assessment of identified differences
- Recommendations for contract selection or negotiation points

## Constraints
- Only analyze pricing and coverage sections as specified
- Flag any unclear or ambiguous contract language
- Do not provide legal advice or final contract recommendations
- Maintain confidentiality of all contract information
- Note any missing information that prevents complete comparison
